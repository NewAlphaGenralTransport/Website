Health and Safety Policy

New Alpha General Transports aims to be the leader in transportation related safety performance with continuous measurable improvements towards the goal of an injury free workplace. At NAGT we believe that all accidents are preventable and that senior management is responsible for putting in place the organization and processes to meet this commitment. Through this commitment to excellence in safety we confirm to our employees that we will respect and care for their safety at all times. Occupational Health and Safety Management System which is in accordance with OHSAS 18001 requirements and with corporate guidelines.

Quality Policy

New Alpha General Transports Quality Policy expresses the management's commitment to provide our clients and society with high-quality Heavy Machine Renting products and related services in an efficient and stable manner. NAGT strive to achieve this in its everyday transportation activities through implementation of the plan-do-check-act (PDCA) process and the Quality Management System which is in accordance with ISO 9001 requirements and with corporate guidelines

Environment Policy

New Alpha General Transports Environmental Policy highlights the importance of environmental management and conveys the management’s commitment to protect and enhance the environment. High environmental performance is an ongoing priority and is achieved by our actions in line with the environmental policy. Our goal is to minimize the negative impacts of our operations and maximize the quality of the built environment for future generations.
